    1. Longitude: -180 to 180 (east positive). Latitude: -90 to 90 (north positive).
    2. All resultes are in the prophetic Gregorian calendar
    3. Here the bazi is assumed to be in local apparent time (LAT, 真太陽時)
    4. The finding date & time are displayed in local mean time (LMT, 平太陽時)
    5. Longitude is essential to the calculation.
